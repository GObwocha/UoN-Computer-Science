Incident desk

Report a county or national environmental issue for review by the responsible office or national coordination desk.

When to use this service

  • Pollution, illegal dumping, wetland encroachment and water contamination
  • Flood alerts, disaster response and urgent cross-county environmental incidents
  • Reports routed through all 47 county offices and the national coordination location

Guidance

What reporters should include

  • Choose the county office or the national disaster coordination centre
  • Enter the exact incident place and nearest landmark
  • What happened and when it was seen
  • Whether people, water points or wildlife are affected
  • Enough detail for county officers to verify the case
